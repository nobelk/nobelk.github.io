Tips to improve your resumes for engineering jobs

home | photography | software engineering | talks | About

  • Resume should not be longer than 1 to 2 pages
  • Limit what you add in the resume - this is not an essay contest
  • Highlight your Honors/awards list
  • Focus on your professional service (not hobbies, interests, ...)
  • Highlight your professional service/job related achievements:
    For example, our developed software has been used by ... to solve ... resulting in ...
  • Highlight your ability to work with others within and outside your team
  • Highlight your efficiency with tools/languages - SQL, C# only mention the languages that you can fluently write code in
  • Use strong verbs: e.g., resulted in improvements, yielded performance improvements, optimized .... etc.
  • Mention your online profiles: github, linkedin, etc.
  • Be very specific/accurate - if you join a company, usually they will do background checks on you based on your resume.
  • Limit the list of your publications to your most significant ones
  • Refrain from adding personal information (e.g., birthday)
  • Sites such as enhancv.com may help you format the resume better
  • Hiring a professional resume writer may be helpful
